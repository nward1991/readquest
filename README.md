# ReadQuest — put it on your phone in ~10 minutes

## Step 1 — Get the app online (one time, free)
1. Go to **github.com** and sign up for a free account (if you don't have one).
2. Click the **+** in the top-right → **New repository**.
3. Name it `readquest`, keep it **Public**, click **Create repository**.
4. On the new page, click the **"uploading an existing file"** link.
5. Drag ALL the files in this folder (index.html, manifest.webmanifest, sw.js, and the 3 png icons) into the upload box → click **Commit changes**.
6. Go to **Settings** (tab at top) → **Pages** (left sidebar).
7. Under "Branch", pick **main**, folder **/ (root)** → **Save**.
8. Wait ~2 minutes, refresh the page — GitHub shows your app's address:
   `https://YOURNAME.github.io/readquest/`

## Step 2 — Install it on your phone
- **iPhone:** open that address in Safari → tap the Share button → **Add to Home Screen** → Add.
- **Android:** open it in Chrome → tap ⋮ menu → **Add to Home screen** (or "Install app").

That's it. ReadQuest now has an icon on your phone and opens full-screen like a native app.

## Notes
- First AI portrait: a Puter permission popup appears once — tap Continue.
- The app works offline (logging, XP, avatars in vector mode). Book search and AI portraits need internet.
- Your progress is saved on each device separately.
- To update the app later: upload the new index.html to the same repository (replace the old one).
